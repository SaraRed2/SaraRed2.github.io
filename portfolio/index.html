About me

My name is Sara and I am 19 years old. I was born and raised in Belgrade. I’ve always been interested in how things work, from ordinary children’s toy cars to complex machines today. Knowing that the future is in technology, I was most interested in computers and the entire architecture of their work. Following my curiosity I enrolled in and graduated from the "Nikola Tesla" high school here in Belgrade. After graduating from high school, I acquired knowledge in the field of computer network administration, so I decided to continue on that path and enroll in the ICT College. Although I'm mostly intrested in network administration I am, as of this year, freelancing in the field of web and graphic design trying my best to improve my work and skills each day.
